Financial Aid & Scholarships

We are committed to making education accessible to all qualified students. Our comprehensive financial aid program includes scholarships, grants, loans, and work-study opportunities.

Merit Scholarships

Based on academic achievement, leadership, and other accomplishments. Awards range from $5,000 to full tuition.

Need-Based Grants

Awarded based on demonstrated financial need as determined by the FAFSA or CSS Profile.

Athletic Scholarships

Available for student-athletes in NCAA Division I sports programs.

The priority deadline for financial aid applications is February 1.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the average GPA for admitted students?
The average GPA for our most recent incoming class was 3.8 on a 4.0 scale. However, we consider applications holistically, taking into account many factors beyond GPA.
Are standardized tests required?
For the 2025-2026 academic year, we have a test-optional policy. Students may choose whether to submit SAT or ACT scores as part of their application.
Can I visit the campus before applying?
Yes! We encourage prospective students to visit our campus. We offer guided tours, information sessions, and opportunities to sit in on classes throughout the year.
How do I check my application status?
You can check your application status through our online applicant portal. After submitting your application, you will receive login credentials via email.
Is housing guaranteed for first-year students?
Yes, housing is guaranteed for all first-year students. We have a variety of residence halls and living-learning communities to choose from.
